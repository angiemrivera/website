about us

The Ethical Exchange was created by two college students aiming to help others believe they can make an IMPACT in our environment one fabric at a time.

Starting with local clothes swaps at their campus, our student team has evolved with the passion to help the broader D.C. community find the easiest, most affordable, and give options to people to encourage sustainable living and practices.

we couldn’t have done it without them...

At The Ethical Exchange, we are thankful for the contribution of UI Designer Angie Rivera, along with her fellow classmates in GDES-315 + professor, Yana Sakellion. This website is part of the ‘Earth Day’ project, which focuses on the specific issue of Fast Fashion, and highlighting solutions meant for it.
